## About FantasticWorlds

*Amat Victoria Curam* — Victory favours the prepared.

*Ad Astra Per Aspera* — To the stars through hardships.

This website was put together as a project to catalogue classic sci-fi and horror films from the 1950s through to the 1980s. It covers everything from the campy B-movies of the 50s to the iconic blockbusters of the late 70s.

The site is a hobby developed in spare time. No movies are sold here.
